Syllabus

Module 1: Laplace Transform

7 hours – Laplace transforms, properties, inverse Laplace, unit step and Dirac delta functions, convolution theorem.

7 hours – Solving ODEs and PDEs using Laplace transforms.

Module 3: Fourier Series

6 hours – Periodic functions, Dirichlet conditions, Fourier series, even/odd function expansions.

Module 4: Fourier Transform

6 hours – Fourier transform, inverse, properties, and applications.

Module 5: Z-Transform

6 hours – Definition, properties, inverse, and applications to difference equations.
