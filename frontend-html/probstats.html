Syllabus

Module 1: Introduction to Statistics

6 hours – Statistics and data analysis; Measures of central tendency and dispersion; Moments, Skewness, Kurtosis (Concepts only).

Module 2: Random Variables

8 hours – PMF, PDF, joint distributions; expectation, covariance, MGF, marginal and conditional distributions.

Module 3: Correlation and Regression

4 hours – Rank correlation, partial & multiple correlation; multiple regression models.

Module 4: Probability Distributions

7 hours – Binomial, Poisson, Normal, Gamma, Exponential, Weibull distributions.

Module 5: Hypothesis Testing I

4 hours – Z-tests for proportions and means; testing procedure, errors, critical region.

Module 6: Hypothesis Testing II

9 hours – t-test, F-test, chi-square, ANOVA (one-way, two-way, three-way), CRD, RBD, LSD.

Module 7: Reliability

5 hours – Hazard function, system reliability, maintainability, availability, preventive and repair maintenance.
