Syllabus

Module 1: Single Variable Calculus

8 hours – Differentiation: Extrema on an Interval, Rolle’s and Mean value theorems, increasing/decreasing functions, first and second derivative tests, maxima and minima, concavity. Integration: average value, area between curves, volumes of solids of revolution.

Module 2: Multivariable Calculus

5 hours – Functions of two variables, limits and continuity, partial derivatives, total differential, Jacobian and its properties.

Module 3: Application of Multivariable Calculus

5 hours – Taylor’s expansion for two variables, maxima and minima, constrained maxima and minima using Lagrange’s multiplier method.

Module 4: Multiple Integrals

8 hours – Double integrals, change of order and variables (Cartesian ↔ Polar), triple integrals, change of variables between Cartesian, cylindrical, and spherical coordinates.

Module 5: Special Functions

6 hours – Gamma and Beta functions, relation between them. Error function. Standard integrals involving Gamma and Beta.

Module 6: Vector Differentiation

5 hours – Gradient, divergence, and curl. Directional derivatives. Solenoidal and irrotational vector fields.

Module 7: Vector Integration

6 hours – Line, surface, and volume integrals. Green’s theorem, Stokes’ theorem, Gauss’s divergence theorem and applications.
